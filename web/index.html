WY CHOOSE US?

ABOUT US

Delicious Dance Academy, established in Oman since 2012, has begun its journey with the vision of nourishing the field of fine arts by fostering a community that encourages creativity, innovation, artistic growth and personal discovery. With an incredible strength of more than 500 students and well-trained faculties, DDA is now the most sought-after dance institute in Oman with an aim to provide quality training in various dance forms and making it available to diverse age groups.
